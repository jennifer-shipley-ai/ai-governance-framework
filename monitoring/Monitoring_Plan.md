# AI Monitoring Plan

A plan for ongoing monitoring of AI systems in production.

---

## 1. Performance Monitoring

- Track key metrics (accuracy, precision, recall, etc.)  
- Compare against baselines  
- Alert on degradation  

---

## 2. Data & Drift Monitoring

- Monitor input data distributions  
- Detect drift over time  
- Trigger retraining or review  

---

## 3. Operational Monitoring

- Uptime and latency  
- Error rates  
- Dependency health  

---

## 4. Risk & Incident Monitoring

- Log incidents and anomalies  
- Classify severity  
- Escalate to governance teams  

---

## 5. Compliance & Audit

- Maintain logs and audit trails  
- Periodic reviews  
- Evidence for regulatory or internal audits  

---

## 6. Reporting

- Regular dashboards for stakeholders  
- Executive summaries  
- Action plans for issues found  

---

This plan ensures AI systems remain safe, effective, and aligned with governance expectations.
