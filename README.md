# AI Governance Framework

A practical governance framework for managing AI systems responsibly across their lifecycle.  
This repo demonstrates how I approach model lifecycle, risk, approvals, responsible AI, and monitoring at an enterprise level.

---

## 📁  What's Inside

- **Model lifecycle governance**  — Defines the end‑to‑end model lifecycle, including documentation requirements, review checkpoints, deployment criteria, and retirement workflows. Shows how you operationalize governance from ideation through decommissioning.
- **AI risk identification and mitigation** — Provides a structured AI risk assessment model with categories, scoring, and mitigation strategies. Demonstrates how you evaluate model impact, likelihood, and required controls.
- **Approval workflows and decision gates** — Outlines decision gates, required artifacts, stakeholder roles, and approval sequencing. Highlights how enterprise teams move models through compliant release processes.
- **Responsible AI principles** — Codifies responsible AI principles and maps them to actionable delivery behaviors. Shows how fairness, transparency, privacy, and accountability are embedded into your governance approach.
- **Monitoring and ongoing oversight**  — Details post‑deployment monitoring, drift detection, alerting thresholds, retraining triggers, and compliance reporting. Demonstrates how you ensure ongoing oversight and model health.

---

## 🎯 Why This Matters

AI governance is the backbone of safe, scalable AI adoption.  
This framework shows how I:

- Align AI initiatives with policy and risk appetite  
- Ensure models are documented, reviewed, and monitored  
- Embed responsible AI principles into delivery  
- Coordinate legal, risk, security, and business stakeholders
