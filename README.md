# AI Governance Framework

A practical governance framework for managing AI systems responsibly across their lifecycle.  
This repo demonstrates how I approach model lifecycle, risk, approvals, responsible AI, and monitoring at an enterprise level.

---

## 📁  What's Inside

- **Model lifecycle governance**  — End‑to‑end model lifecycle stages, required artifacts, and governance checkpoints.
- **AI risk identification and mitigation** — Simple scoring model for AI risks with categories and mitigation actions.
- **Approval workflows and decision gates** — Decision gates, required documentation, and stakeholder roles for model release.
- **Responsible AI principles** — Core responsible AI principles translated into practical delivery expectations.
- **Monitoring and ongoing oversight** — Post‑deployment monitoring, drift detection, alerts, and retraining triggers.

---

## 🎯 Why This Matters

AI governance is the backbone of safe, scalable AI adoption.  
This framework shows how I:

- Align AI initiatives with policy and risk appetite  
- Ensure models are documented, reviewed, and monitored  
- Embed responsible AI principles into delivery  
- Coordinate legal, risk, security, and business stakeholders
