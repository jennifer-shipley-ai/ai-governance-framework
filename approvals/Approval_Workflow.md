# AI Approval Workflow

A structured workflow for approving AI models and solutions.

---

## 1. Submission

- Use case summary  
- Model documentation  
- Data sources and lineage  
- Risk assessment  

---

## 2. Review Steps

- Technical review (architecture, performance)  
- Data review (quality, privacy, sensitivity)  
- Security review (threats, controls)  
- Legal/compliance review  

---

## 3. Governance Committee

- Present use case and risk profile  
- Discuss mitigations and controls  
- Decide: Approve, Approve with conditions, or Reject  

---

## 4. Conditions & Actions

- Required monitoring thresholds  
- Additional controls or audits  
- Limited rollout or pilot phase  

---

## 5. Final Approval & Record

- Decision recorded  
- Approvers documented  
- Conditions tracked  
- Artifacts stored in a central repository  

---

This workflow ensures AI solutions are reviewed and approved consistently.
