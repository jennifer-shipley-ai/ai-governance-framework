# AI Risk Matrix

A simple matrix to categorize and manage AI risks.

---

## Risk Categories

- Data risk  
- Model risk  
- Operational risk  
- Compliance risk  
- Ethical risk  

---

## Example Risk Matrix

| Risk ID | Category       | Likelihood | Impact  | Rating | Mitigation                          |
|--------|----------------|-----------|--------|--------|--------------------------------------|
| R1     | Data           | Medium    | High   | High   | Improve data quality, add controls   |
| R2     | Model Bias     | Low       | High   | Medium | Bias testing, human review           |
| R3     | Security       | Medium    | High   | High   | Harden APIs, access controls         |
| R4     | Compliance     | Low       | Very High | High | Legal review, policy alignment    |
| R5     | Operational    | High      | Medium | High   | Redundancy, monitoring, runbooks     |

---

## PM Responsibilities

- Maintain risk register  
- Coordinate mitigations  
- Escalate high‑risk items  
- Ensure governance reviews occur  

---

This matrix provides a repeatable way to track and manage AI risks.
