# Model Lifecycle Governance

A structured view of how AI models move from idea to retirement under governance.

---

## 1. Ideation & Use Case Definition

- Business problem defined  
- Value hypothesis documented  
- Stakeholders identified  
- Risk and compliance considerations captured  

---

## 2. Data Assessment

- Data sources identified  
- Data quality evaluated  
- Privacy and sensitivity assessed  
- Access controls defined  

---

## 3. Model Design & Development

- Model objectives defined  
- Features selected  
- Training and validation approach documented  
- Bias and fairness testing planned  

---

## 4. Validation & Approval

- Performance metrics reviewed  
- Risk assessment completed  
- Legal and compliance review  
- Governance committee sign‑off  

---

## 5. Deployment

- Release plan approved  
- Rollback strategy defined  
- Access and security configured  
- User communication prepared  

---

## 6. Monitoring & Maintenance

- Performance monitoring  
- Drift detection  
- Incident management  
- Retraining schedule  

---

## 7. Retirement

- Decommission plan  
- Data retention and archiving  
- Documentation updated  
- Stakeholders informed  

---

This lifecycle ensures AI models are governed from concept to retirement.
